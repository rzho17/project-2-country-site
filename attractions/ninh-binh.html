Ninh Binh

Filming spot for Kong: Skull Island.

"Where mountains meet rice fields in serene harmony.”"

- C. Nguyen

Often called "Hạ Long Bay on land," Ninh Bình stuns visitors with limestone karsts rising from lush rice paddies and winding rivers. It’s a peaceful escape rich in natural beauty and ancient temples.
