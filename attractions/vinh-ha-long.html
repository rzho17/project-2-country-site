Vịnh Hạ Long

Named after a “Descending Dragon.”

"Sail through a dreamscape of limestone wonders."

- C. Nguyen

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ha Long Bay is famous for its emerald waters and thousands of towering limestone islands. It’s a must-see destination for cruising, kayaking, and surreal landscapes.
